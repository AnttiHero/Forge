# Vulcan Industrial Partners III, L.P. — Summary of Principal Terms

**The Fund.** Vulcan Industrial Partners III, L.P., a Delaware limited partnership.

**Target Size.** $3,000,000,000 in aggregate commitments; hard cap $3,500,000,000.

**Strategy.** Control buyouts of industrial businesses. Primary focus North America and Europe; the General Partner is considering an emerging-markets basket of up to 15% of commitments.

**Sponsor Commitment.** The General Partner and its affiliates will commit at least 2.5% of aggregate commitments.

**Investment Period.** Five years from final closing.

**Term.** Ten years, plus two one-year extensions with Advisory Board consent.

**Management Fee.** 2.0% per annum on commitments during the Investment Period; 1.5% on invested capital thereafter. 100% fee offset for transaction, monitoring and directors' fees.

**Carried Interest.** 20% over an 8% preferred return, full catch-up, European (whole-of-fund) waterfall, GP clawback with personal guarantees.

**Key Persons.** Three named principals; suspension if fewer than three devote substantially all business time; reinstatement by 66 2/3% LP vote.

**Co-Investment.** Offered pro rata to LPs with commitments of $75,000,000 or more; no fee, no carry.

**MFN.** Compendium to LPs at or above $75,000,000; 30-day election window.

**Reporting.** Audited annuals within 90 days; quarterlies within 45 days; ILPA-format capital account statements.

**Transfers.** GP consent required; pre-approved for wholly-owned affiliates.

**Diversification.** No more than 20% of commitments in a single portfolio company; no more than 15% outside primary regions without Advisory Board consent.
